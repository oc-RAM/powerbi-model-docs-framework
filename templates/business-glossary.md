> Define shared business terms used across this model and its reports. The purpose of this glossary is to prevent definition drift - the gradual divergence in how different teams interpret the same term.
>
> Add a term here when:
> - It appears in measures, KPIs, or report labels
> - Different stakeholders might interpret it differently
> - It has a specific meaning in this context that differs from casual usage
> - It was the subject of a definition disagreement or clarification

> **Term**: The business term as used in reports and conversations.
>
> **Definition**: A precise, unambiguous definition. Write it so that someone unfamiliar with the project would understand exactly what it means.
>
> **Source of Truth**: Where the authoritative definition comes from (SLA contract, business owner, policy document, etc.).
>
> **Related Measures**: Which measures in the semantic model use or depend on this term.
>
> **Owner**: The person or role responsible for maintaining this definition.
>
> **Last Reviewed**: When this definition was last confirmed as current and accurate.
